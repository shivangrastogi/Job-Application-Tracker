// what's inside

Everything the job grind needs — nothing it doesn't.

01 · live sync

Career pages, decoded.

Add a company and AppliTrack reads its careers page through the ATS underneath — Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday, Amazon Jobs and more — then normalises every listing so you can filter by role, location and remote.

GreenhouseLeverAshbyWorkdayAmazonSmartRecruiters
02 · goals

100 a day? Done.

Set daily, weekly or monthly targets. Live progress bars track applications logged, submitted and interviews — with a nightly nudge so you never break the streak.

03 · referrals

Never lose a referral.

Save your referral groups and Google Forms, log each request, and pre-fill forms with your details in one tap. Convert a referral straight into a tracked application.

04 · analytics

See your funnel.

Wishlist → Applied → Phone → Tech → Onsite → Offer. Watch your conversion, response rate and where applications stall — so you fix the leak, not the symptom.

05 · alerts

New jobs, the second they post.

AppliTrack quietly checks your tracked companies and pings you when fresh openings appear — no refresh, no FOMO.

06 · private

Local-first. Your data.

Everything lives on your device by default, with optional cloud sync. No résumé farms, no recruiter spam — just your hunt, your way.

// the pipeline
